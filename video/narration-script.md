# MACD Divergence Narration Script (English)

## Scene 1: Intro (0:00 - 0:05)
"Welcome to this complete guide on MACD Divergence — a powerful technical analysis tool used to identify potential reversals and continuations in financial markets."

## Scene 2: What is Divergence? (0:05 - 0:10)
"MACD Divergence occurs when price and the MACD oscillator move in opposite directions. There are two main types: Regular Divergence, which signals a potential reversal, and Hidden Divergence, which signals trend continuation."

## Scene 3: Bullish Divergence (0:10 - 0:15)
"Bullish Divergence appears in downtrends. When price makes lower lows but the MACD oscillator makes higher lows, it means sellers are losing momentum. This is a BUY signal — expect a reversal to the upside."

## Scene 4: Bearish Divergence (0:15 - 0:20)
"Bearish Divergence appears in uptrends. When price makes higher highs but the MACD oscillator makes lower highs, it means buyers are losing momentum. This is a SELL signal — expect a reversal to the downside."

## Scene 5: Hidden Bullish Divergence (0:20 - 0:25)
"Hidden Bullish Divergence appears during uptrend pullbacks. When price makes higher lows but the MACD makes lower lows, it signals the uptrend is resuming. This is a continuation BUY signal."

## Scene 6: Hidden Bearish Divergence (0:25 - 0:30)
"Hidden Bearish Divergence appears during downtrend bounces. When price makes lower highs but the MACD makes higher highs, it signals the downtrend is resuming. This is a continuation SELL signal."

## Scene 7: Outro (0:30 - 0:35)
"Remember these key rules: Regular divergence means reversal, hidden means continuation. Higher timeframes are stronger. Always wait for MACD crossover confirmation, and maintain a minimum risk-reward ratio of one to two."
